Our Background

Established in Seattle, Washington in 1992 by Johnny William, KAPE is known and valued for providing a more pleasant coffee shop experience that delivers handcrafted barista drinks and the distinctly smooth, deep-roasted coffee flavor of the Pacific Northwest. Johnny William, our founder was originally a Seattle based real estate developer and investor. His swoop into the real estate business came soon after college when he and a friend wrote a business plan to start an upscale neighborhood gourmet grocery store featuring domestic and international artisan produced food and beverage products.
